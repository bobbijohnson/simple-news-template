    Traveling fitness groups

    Do you love to travel and stay fit? Why not mix the two! Are there fitness groups out there? The World would like to know? We all need a little motivation, some of us need just a bit more. It is important that we stay healthy and fit so we can live a long, happy, and successful life! Q: Is there a such thing as a traveling fitness group? If so are they popular? A: There is no such thing as travelling fitness groups. There are places you could go and teach the classes but not go as a group. Hopefully in the near future someone will start a fitness travelling group that goes around hiking, swimming, and more! That would be awesome! There are places that you can go and join a class in your local area. But, there is not a fitness group that travels. Q: How are the classes organized? Are they seperated by age? A: There are a variety of classes you can take. Some classes are split up by age group and others are just on your capability to do things. A list of some classes are: Active Older Adults, which consist of water aerobics and yoga. Cardio Fitness, which consist of kickboxing, outdoor bootcamp, and aerobics. Dance, which is Zumba, Hip-hop, and Pop fitness. They also have classes for kids and family.
