How to make your life just A little easier by Jordan Brooks

  1. Home
  2. Lifestyle

Posted On May 31, 2018

Have you ever just wanted to make your daily life just a little easier?Or just to get through the day easier? Well here are 5 tips/ life hacks to make that happen for you.

Hack One : Do you ever feel stressed or just need a way to relax your body. Take a day for yourself and run a hot bath with some bath salts or a bath bomb and turn on your favorite music station. Mute your phone and just let the music relax your mind.

Tip One: want to exercise without having to really exercise when you clean up different rooms in your house turn on music that gets your moving and just got from there and clean up.

Hack Two: Do you ever feel stressed at work or home and just need some ways to help you calm your nerves. When you start feeling stressed you should take a nap or take a break from what you're doing and just go for a walk

Tip Two: Do you need a easy on the go meal so that your not feeling tired or missing any meals. Plan out a week's worth of meals on the weekend and cook one meal that will last you all week (you can add in things like fruit and vegetables as well)

Hack Three: Do you ever forget to grab your keep or forget to take your medicine. Just put something like your work bag or cell phone next to it and when you see your phone you will see whatever it is you need to take with you
