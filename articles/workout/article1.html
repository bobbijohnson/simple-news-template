Here are some basic exercises you can do to stay in shape. Here are Five basic exercises and how to do them. Bodyweight squats: Start with your feet slightly apart about 5-15 degrees. -Put your hips back toward the wall real it you need help and if you not than imagine one bend your knees into a lower squat. -Keep your weight on your heels and your chest up and keep your knees inline with your feet. -Go as low as you can than push through your heels to return to standing. Squats can help your butt grow and your quads/ thighs grow but get it wrong it's a great exercise for the whole body but if your focusing on your butt and thighs do squats.

Pushups: First start at a high plank position place your palms under your shoulders or wider whatever your prefer *Keep your fingers pointing directly forward -Keep your core tight and bend elbows to your lower torso toward the floor. -Don't let your hips drop down low before your chest lowers. -Go as low as you can push through and straighten your arms. Push ups will help you get your upper body stronger this will also help you gain abs and a flat belly.

The Burpee: Stand with your feet hip-distance apart. Then bring your palms to the floor -Jump your feet back so that you are in high plank,keeping your core tight and your hips lifted. -Bend your elbows and than do a push-up going back into a high plank. -Jump to your feet to the outside of your hands and come up. When you do burpees it helps you work your arms ork your arms, chest, quads, glutes,hamstrings, and abs. If your doing them right you should feel a little bit of pain in your legs.

Pull Ups: Stand under the bar, palms on the bar, hands slightly wider than your shoulders. Activate your core -Pull yourself up so that your chin reaches the bar. Keep your posture straight. -Slowly lower yourself down, so that your arms are straight and your feet do not touch the floor or bench -Start with five reps, two sets, two to three times a week, increase slowly. Benefits with doing pull ups is that it improve posture it will help strengthen your arms and it will strengthen your back and help you look better physically.
