How to: Planned Eating by Bobbi Johnson

  1. Home
  2. Blog Post

May 2018

What is planned eating? Planned eating is cooking in advanced. More commonly known as meal prep. It is where you cook a mass amount of food at the beginning of the week. You are eating a prepared meal for more than 2 days. Like your mom making your breakfast lunch and dinner. You’ll make like steak and chicken with broccoli and other things and eat it through the week.

How is this beneficial? This is great for those who want to lose weight because you won’t have a lot of snacks and things that are bad for you, you won’t be tempted to eat those things if you have a whole meal in your fridge!

Will you save? This is 10000000% an amazing way to save money! You don’t have to spend money everyday or so to buy groceries or take out. You have a meal nearly everyday of the week. This also saves time, which everyone knows saves money. Imagine you come home after a long day, it’s late, there’s nothing cooking; and you don’t feel like cooking, you barely have anything in the fridge to make that well known struggle meal. You don’t wanna break your diet, what do you do? You heat up or toss up that meal you prepared on Sunday, a few days ago, you go to bed full and happy. Crisis averted.
