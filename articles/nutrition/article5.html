What's The Best Nutrition App by Bobbi Johnson

  1. Home
  2. Blog Post

Posted on August 24, 2013 at 9:00 PM

    What kinds of Apps are out there? MyFitnessPal Lifesum Lose it! FatSecret Cron-o-meter SparkPeople
There are many more, but these are the most raved about, you can try any you would like, it’s based on what you like.

What are they monitoring? These app monitor your calorie intake, what you eat throughout the day, your health levels over a course of time. Your weight, water consumption. They can monitor; if you are a women, your cycle, because it is a fact that during your period you can gain as much as 10 pounds! If you are trying to lose weight, it can account for that and help you get to your goal as well. These things can also monitor how much exercise you do each day, how long you work out and your work out regimine.

So which is the best? In all honest, is it preference because the apps do have different features that aren’t on the other apps. From personal experience, I enjoy Lifesum, for me it really helps me keep track of my weight, and how much I eat on a daily, making sure I am healthy based on my age and height.
